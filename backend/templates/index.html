Core Features

Data Processing

Cleans and structures raw vehicle data. Organizes engine sizes, fuel consumption, and transmission types into a machine-learning-ready format.

CO2 Prediction

Uses an optimized XGBoost algorithm to estimate the exact grams of CO2 a vehicle will emit per kilometer based on your form inputs.

Efficiency Scoring (VEEI)

Calculates the custom Vehicle Emission Efficiency Index. This score from 0 to 100 tells you how efficient a car is for its specific size and class.
